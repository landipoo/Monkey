About Monkeys

What Are Monkeys?

Monkeys are fascinating primates found across the globe. Known for their intelligence, social behavior, and agility, they belong to the *Cercopithecoidea* (Old World monkeys) and *Platyrrhini* (New World monkeys) families. They come in all shapes and sizes, from the tiny pygmy marmoset to the robust mandrill.

The Importance of Monkeys

Monkeys play a vital role in maintaining ecological balance. As seed dispersers, they help forests regenerate. Their social structures and behaviors also provide insights into human evolution and social dynamics.

Our Mission

This website is dedicated to sharing knowledge and fostering appreciation for these amazing animals. By exploring the world of monkeys, we hope to inspire conservation efforts and deeper understanding.
