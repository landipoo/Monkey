These are some uniqe traits about monkeys.

  • Smart Primates: Monkeys are known for their intelligence. Capuchins are particularly famous for their problem-solving skills.
  • Communication: Monkeys use a variety of vocalizations, facial expressions, and gestures to communicate with each other.
  • Color Vision: Old World monkeys have trichromatic color vision (like humans), while many New World monkeys are dichromatic (limited color perception).
  • Thumbs: Some monkeys, like baboons, have opposable thumbs, while others, like spider monkeys, do not.
  • Life Span: Monkeys can live from 10 to 50 years depending on the species.
